Why I Traded Software for Real Estate

Since 2018, I have been building complex technology products in the high-stakes worlds of finance and government technology. In those environments, 'close enough' isn't good enough. Security, precision, and integrity were the non-negotiable baseline.

Real estate is no different. Whether you're buying or selling, the stakes are high and the variables are many. Modern real estate agents simply lack the tools and training to navigate this complexity effectively. Even seasoned agents rely on intuition and anecdote over data and evidence, leading to missed opportunities and suboptimal outcomes. I founded my practice to bridge the gap between traditional real estate and modern data science.

Being 'data-driven' isn't a buzzword for me--it's a commitment to using rigorous analysis, predictive modeling, and evidence-based strategiesb> to guide every decision. I build custom models to analyze market trends, optimize pricing strategies, and evaluate offer strength. This approach allows me to provide clients with clear, actionable insights rather than vague assurances.

I've spent my career navigating complex systems. I have worked as a data scientist, and senior software engineer, for Fortune 500 companies, and in 2026 I earned my Masters in Computer Science, where I explored advanced algorithms and machine learning techniques. Now, I apply that expertise to real estate, helping clients make informed decisions based on data, not guesswork.

My promise: I'll bring the same rigor forged in my software career to your real estate journey. I will advocate for your best interests with clarity, precision, and calm guidance. I will represent you with integrity, backed by data you can trust.
